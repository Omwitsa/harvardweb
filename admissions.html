Accommodation


ihti has partnered with Qwetu, a private provider of modern student accommodation. They currently have a choice of three locations one on Jogoo road, Parklands and another on in Madaraka.

Qwetu is a new kind of student living experience, designed with international students in mind. They are the number one choice for student accommodation because they genuinely care about how you want to live. Living with Qwetu isn’t just about buildings, it’s about safe, secure homes where you can study and socialise in comfort.

Some of the facilities Qwetu student accommodation offers include:

  • Wifi
  • Private Gym
  • Social Lounges
  • Study Areas
  • Security
  • Borehole water
  • Standby Generator
  • Shuttle Transport

Please book your room through their website: http://qwetu.co.ke/
