Our Profile

Harvard's Profile

Havard Institute of Development Studies is located in the heart of Thika town on the fifth floor of victory plaza. We opened our doors on 4th of January 2011 with about 4 students taking a course in early childhood studies. The number has since risen to include many other courses offered like computer studies, ICT, Business management, community development, social work, Electrical engineering, computer engineering and many others.

Our Vision

To be a hub for professional and market oriented graduates for international and local community growth.


Our Mission

To provide, upgrade and enhance quality training and education for community growth and development.
